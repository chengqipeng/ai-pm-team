## 概述

老系统通过 `MetaConstants.METAMODEL_ID_*` 常量定义元模型 ID（负数 Long），每个元模型对应一个 DTO 类（X 前缀）和一个 PO 类（数据库实体）。实体域元模型共 28 个，按层级关系分为核心元模型和子元模型。

## 层级关系图

```
entity（对象）METAMODEL_ID_OBJECT
  ├── item（字段）METAMODEL_ID_ITEM
  │     ├── pickOption（选项值）METAMODEL_ID_PICK_OPTION
  │     ├── referenceFilter（关联过滤）METAMODEL_ID_REFERENCE_FILTER
  │     │     └── referenceFilterDetail（过滤条件明细）METAMODEL_ID_REFERENCE_FILTER_DETAIL
  │     ├── formulaCompute（计算公式）METAMODEL_ID_FORMULA_COMPUTE
  │     │     ├── formulaComputeItem（公式引用字段）METAMODEL_ID_FORMULA_COMPUTE_ITEM
  │     │     └── computeFactor（计算因子）METAMODEL_ID_COMPUTE_FACTOR
  │     ├── aggregationCompute（汇总累计）METAMODEL_ID_AGGREGATION_COMPUTE
  │     │     ├── aggregationComputeDetail（汇总条件）METAMODEL_ID_AGGREGATION_COMPUTE_DETAIL
  │     │     └── computeFactor（计算因子，共享）
  │     ├── itemDependency（字段依赖）METAMODEL_ID_ITEM_DEPENDENCY
  │     │     └── itemDependencyDetail（依赖明细）METAMODEL_ID_ITEM_DEPENDENCY_DETAIL
  │     └── fieldSetItem（字段集字段）METAMODEL_ID_FIELD_SET_ITEM
  ├── entityLink（关联关系）METAMODEL_ID_XOBJECT_LINK
  ├── checkRule（校验规则）METAMODEL_ID_CHECK_RULE
  ├── duplicateRule（查重规则）METAMODEL_ID_DUPLICATE_RULE
  │     ├── duplicateRuleCriteria（查重条件）METAMODEL_ID_DUPLICATE_RULE_CRITERIA
  │     └── duplicateMatchingRule（匹配规则）METAMODEL_ID_DUPLICATE_MATCHING_RULE
  ├── busiType（业务类型）METAMODEL_ID_BUSI_TYPE
  │     ├── busiTypeMapping（类型映射）METAMODEL_ID_BUSITYPE_MAPPING
  │     │     └── busiTypeMappingDetail（映射明细）METAMODEL_ID_BUSITYPE_MAPPING_DETAIL
  │     └── busiTypePickOption（类型选项值）METAMODEL_ID_BUSITYPE_PICKOPTION
  └── fieldSet（字段集）METAMODEL_ID_FIELD_SET
        └── fieldSetItem（字段集字段）METAMODEL_ID_FIELD_SET_ITEM

globalPickItem（全局选项集）METAMODEL_ID_GLOBAL_PICK_ITEM
  ├── globalPickOption（全局选项值）METAMODEL_ID_GLOBAL_PICK_OPTION
  ├── globalPickDependency（全局依赖）METAMODEL_ID_GLOBAL_PICK_DEPENDENCY
  │     └── globalPickDependencyDetail（依赖明细）METAMODEL_ID_GLOBAL_PICK_DEPENDENCY_DETAIL
  └── pickOption（选项值，共享）METAMODEL_ID_PICK_OPTION
```
